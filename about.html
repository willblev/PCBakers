Welcome to the PCBakers website!

Charity bake sales are a tried-and-tested method to raise money for good causes while simultaneously enjoying delicious food and new meeting people who you otherwise might not see on your daily routine.

Upcoming bake sales will be announced via the PCB internal newsletter T'Interessa, as well as on the digital screens and on the billboards throughout the parc.

The PCBakers is an offshoot of the Incubakers, an organization which began in 2013 at the PRBB.

How does it work?

Any individual or group from the PCB can propose a cause by contacting the PCBakers Organizers. After the PCBakers Organizers coordinate with you to confirm a date for your proposed charity bake sale, then you are responsible for organizing and running the bake sale on that date. At the end of each bake sale, we announce how much money was raised and send 100% of the donations to the charity.

Sound complicated? Don't worry! There is an easy-to-follow guide called the "PCBakers protocol" to ensure that each event runs smoothly, and the PCBakers Organizers are always available to answer your questions.
