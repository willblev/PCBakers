Contact

If you want to propose a charity for a future bake sale, join the group as a baker/organizer/volunteer, or be informed of all upcoming events, you can request to join the PCBakers mailing list on this Google Groups page by clicking on "Ask to join group".

For all other questions, you can send us an email at bakerspcb@gmail.com or via our social media accounts (links on the top of this page).
