Contact

If you want to propose a charity for a future bake sale, join the group as a baker/organizer/volunteer, or be informed of all upcoming events, you can request to join the PCBakers mailing list on this Google Groups page, then click the link "Ask to join group".

For any other questions about the PCBakers initiative, please send us an email at bakerspcb@gmail.com or via our social media accounts (see the navigation bar).
